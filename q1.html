...Separating Content from Presentation

Explanation

The strong and emphasis tags are better choice than using the bold and italics tags because they serve to emphasize the importance of words and their meaning while also providing that visual difference. The tags for bold and italics only visually distinguishes text from the regular text, it does not imply a difference or change in the meaning of the sentence. It is also easier to parse through the HTML code and identify text that is tagged with the strong or emphasis tags, compared to the one letter tags for bold or italics which could be easily missed while skimming through code.
